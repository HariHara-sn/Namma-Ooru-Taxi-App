🧭 Core concept: Heading / Azimuth via Sensor Fusion

The “compass-like” behavior comes from calculating the phone’s azimuth angle (rotation around the Z-axis relative to magnetic north or true north).

This relies on:

1. Sensors involved
Magnetometer → detects Earth’s magnetic field (gives north direction)
Accelerometer → detects gravity (used to determine device tilt)
Gyroscope → detects rotation changes (smooths movement)
